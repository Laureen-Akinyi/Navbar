The Tyre

Tyre is a city in Lebanon, one of the oldest continually inhabited cities in the world, though in medieval times for some centuries by just a tiny population.

Tyre existed in the ancient times as a Phoenician city and is currently one of the largest cities in Lebanon with a population of 135,204.
